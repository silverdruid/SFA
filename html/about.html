About Us

SFA is a renowned football academy with qualified and experienced male and female football coaches, and competent medic team. We aim to deliver quality and top-notch football training programme through our technical and tactical training sessions, creating a fun and conducive training platform for the pupils under the ages 4 to 17 years.

Our mandate is to bridge the gaps that cause deficiencies of quality football coaching in various institutes of learning(Schools). We practically bridge that gap, by bringing the game to their doorstep through their schools. We expose the kids to intensive developmental football programmes, which are capable of unleashing the talent in them.

Our Vision

To raise discipline and intelligent football players who will take the game to a professional level, be a successful professional footballer.

Our Mission

We provide professional football coaching services to children and young adults, and creating a platform to develop their God's given talents.
